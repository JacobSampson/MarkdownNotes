## Introduction to Valuation

### Future Value

$F=P_\(1+\frac r m\)^m_N$

Compute using the $\bar r$

$F =P\(1 + \bar r\)^N$

#### **Compounding**

$F=P\*\(1=\frac r m\)^m$

* Value compounds $m$ times over a year

$\bar r=\(1+\frac r m\)^m-1$

* Nominal \(annual\) rate to the effective \(annual\) rate
* Effective Annual Rate \(EAR\)

$P=\frac F {\(1 + \bar r\)^N}$
